At computer information technology education schools, we offer the following courses:

1. Fundamentals of Computer Science (No. CS101)

This course provides beginners with basic concepts and principles of computer science, including data structures, algorithms, operating systems, and programming languages.

2. Software Development (ID: CS202)

This course focuses on the process, methods, and tools of software development, involving knowledge of object-oriented programming, database design, software testing, and maintenance.

3. Network Technology (No. CS303)

This course delves into the fundamental knowledge of computer networks, including network protocols, routing and switching technologies, network security, and introduces how to design and maintain network systems.

4. Data Analysis and Artificial Intelligence (ID: CS404)

This course combines data analysis and artificial intelligence to introduce cutting-edge technologies such as data mining, machine learning, and natural language processing, cultivating students' abilities in data-driven decision-making.

5. Digital Creativity and Media (ID: CS505)

This course focuses on digital creativity and media production, covering fields such as digital image processing, audio editing, animation production, and interactive media design, cultivating students' creative digital media skills.
